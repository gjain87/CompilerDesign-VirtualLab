AIM -

To implement a Lexical Analyser.

Theory -

A lexical analyser is the firse phase of a compiler which is also known as a scanner . It converts the high level program into stream of tokens according to the rules signified by the grammar of the language.It also removes the white space and comments from a program.

Procedure -

Tokens are defined by regular expressions which are constructed by the lexical analyser. A lexeme is a sequence of characters in the source program that matches the pattern for a token & is identified by the lexical analyser as the instance of the token.
